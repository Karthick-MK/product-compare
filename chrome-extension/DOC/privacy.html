CompareXT — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 18, 2026

CompareXT ("the extension") is a browser extension that lets you collect products from supported shopping sites and compare them side by side. This policy explains what data the extension handles.

Data we collect

We do not collect any personal data. The extension has no account, no login, and no analytics. We do not know who you are.

Data the extension stores

When you click "Add to Compare", the extension saves the following about that product on your own device, using your browser's local storage (chrome.storage.local):

  • Product title, price, image URL, rating, and specifications
  • The product's page URL

This data never leaves your browser. It is not sent to us or to any third party. You can delete it at any time with the "Clear All" / "Remove all" buttons, or by removing the extension.

Permissions we request

  • Storage — to save your compare list locally on your device.
  • Host access to supported shopping sites (Amazon and Flipkart) — to read product details on the pages you visit so the extension can build the comparison. The extension runs only on these sites.

Network requests

To fill in full specification tables, the extension may fetch a product's own page from the shopping site you added it from. These requests go directly to that shopping site — never to a server operated by us. We operate no server and receive no data.

Affiliate disclosure

Some "buy" links generated by the extension include an affiliate tag (Amazon Associates). If you purchase through such a link, we may earn a commission at no extra cost to you. This does not change the price you pay and does not involve sharing any of your data.

Third-party sites

The extension operates on third-party shopping sites (such as Amazon and Flipkart). Those sites have their own privacy policies, which govern your activity on them. This policy covers only the extension.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the "Last updated" date above will change. Material changes will be reflected here before they take effect.
